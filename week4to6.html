Week 04

In week 4 we explored instructional/ algorithmic/ generative art. Started working on p5, learnt about the anatomy of a p5 sketch, and played around with p5 random. I worked with 'circle follows mouse', random colour, creating shapes which ranomised colour, doodling with the mouse, etc. We the worked on a group activity to understand the naunces of randomness. We had to create a game which changed the end outcome based on the roll of a dice. We created a set of rules that initated a set of varied results. Our game in particular was based on the properties of colour, shape, position.

Week 06

In week 6 we explored topics such as Data Networks and hw they are commodified, Data Visualisation & 'Physicalisation' and Data visualisation with p5. We played around with p5, creating basic 'for loops', pie charts using data from our phones based on storage, along with randomisation of colour. I also undertook some fun experimentation with doodling, pointillism, spinny text. We then played around with the idea of data visualisation by segregating all items from our bags into specific clusters based on a pattern. I chose space. Physical space, Liminal space, and Virtual Space. We then did the same with a group of people. We chose the topic of 'What we are carrying in our bags .vs. Things that would be useful on the moon'. This taught us how to tell a story using data (LATCH). I also learnt about data farms, and data clouds. This inspired me to call my workbook - #mydatacloud, and even reworked my cursor to be a mini cloud hehehhehe.
